About Me

Senior ML Engineer with 5+ years of production ML experience at Bosch Global Software Technologies, and a deep focus on accessibility AI. Published IEEE researcher in sign language recognition.

That experience shaped everything since: a data-centric philosophy, a production ASL learning platform used by real learners, and a commitment to building AI that actually works for the people who need it most.
